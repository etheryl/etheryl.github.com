What it enables

Approach: A rich feature set but easy to use

Etheryl's complete feature list allows you to get started right away in managing your community. It includes all the important elements so that users can start contributing right away. You can also move onto more advanced uses such as e-mail broadcasting and subscription . We also know that too many features at once are confusing. Getting used to a complex new tool alone can lead to disuse. That's why we've build each feature to be intuitive and to have a standardized navigation.

Standard Features Community Management
  • Homepage: Easy-to-manage blocks
  • News and editorials
  • Recent community and personalized activity updates
  • Moderation at platform and group level
  • Mass “push” e-mailing
  • Ratings and most popular messages
Knowledge Management
  • Tags and classification
  • Transversal search engine
  • Member directory
  • Content broadcasting by e-mail
  • Content subscription
Membership Management
  • Membership application form
  • Approval and validation back-office
  • Management of member status and expiration
Community Features
  • Rich personal profiles (CV, background, etc)
  • Message Board
  • Event calendar
  • Wiki
  • Files
  • Chat
  • Photo album
  • Personal status
  • Messaging
  • Group creation
  • Photo gallery
Structuring
  • Global community
  • Sub-communities (secret, private, and open)
  • Privacy and visibility settings
  • Exporting of data possible to public sites
  • Integration with your database possible
